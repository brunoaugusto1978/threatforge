# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the ThreatForge community a respectful and professional experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- be respectful and constructive;
- focus on technical merit and defensive security outcomes;
- provide clear feedback;
- avoid harassment, personal attacks and discriminatory language;
- respect responsible disclosure practices;
- avoid sharing sensitive, stolen or unauthorized data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or intimidation;
- hate speech or discriminatory comments;
- publishing private information without consent;
- submitting real leaked credentials or stolen data;
- encouraging unauthorized access or abuse;
- using the project to facilitate harmful activity.

## Security Research Conduct

ThreatForge is intended for defensive, educational and authorized research.

Do not use this project to attack third-party systems without permission.

Do not submit exploit data, credentials, malware samples or sensitive personal data unless explicitly authorized and handled safely.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may also block participants who repeatedly violate these expectations.
